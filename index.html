LANParty VC
LAN‑only No accounts

Drop‑in voice for your LAN.

Everyone on the same network automatically lands in the same room. No codes, no servers, just a shared call.

Your mic stays on your device until you join. WebRTC is peer‑to‑peer.

How it works

  • 1. We detect your public IP and assign a room code.
  • 2. Everyone on the same network shares that room.
  • 3. Cards light up when people speak.
Best on desktop Chromium / Firefox.
